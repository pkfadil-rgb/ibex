About ibex

The other half of
psychology

For decades, psychology in India has been shaped by a deficit model — trained to ask what is wrong, what is broken, what needs fixing. Clinical frameworks dominate our universities and our minds.

Positive psychology offers a different question: what is strong, what is possible, what helps people flourish? It is not the absence of clinical care — it is its necessary counterpart.

ibex was built to bring this counterpart to students across India and beyond. Born between Delhi and London, we are creating a space where the science of strengths is not a footnote — it is the foundation.

"The ibex is a mountain goat that climbs near-vertical rock faces to reach mineral salt — terrain no other animal attempts. It does not avoid the steep. It seeks it out."

the ibex principle · strength through difficulty
What we teach

The pillars of
positive psychology

Character Strengths
Discover your 24 VIA character strengths and apply them to live and work with greater meaning and energy.
Wellbeing & PERMA
Build a life with positive emotions, engagement, relationships, meaning, and accomplishment.
Growth Mindset
How beliefs about ability shape every outcome — and how to cultivate a mindset that turns challenge into growth.
Resilience
Build the capacity to adapt, recover, and grow through adversity — essential for practitioners and people alike.
Coaching Psychology
Evidence-based coaching techniques — how to ask powerful questions, hold space, and apply PP in practice.
Purpose & Meaning
Frameworks for finding purpose, clarifying values, and building a life that is not just successful — but meaningful.
